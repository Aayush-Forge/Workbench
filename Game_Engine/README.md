# 🕹️ Game Engine Research

This department is dedicated to exploring the mechanics of **2D and 3D development** with a specific focus on **AI-integrated gameplay**. Here, I prototype the physics, logic, and systems that power immersive digital worlds.

---

### 🔬 Research Focus
* **AI-Driven NPCs:** Integrating **LLMs** and **RAG** into non-player characters so they have memory and reactive personalities.
* **Spatial Systems:** Experimenting with movement, collision, and physics in both 2D and 3D environments.
* **Dynamic Narratives:** Designing systems where player choices and AI interactions change the game's world state in real-time.

---

*"Building worlds where the environment thinks as much as the player."*
